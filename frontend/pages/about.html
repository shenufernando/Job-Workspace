Empowering the Informal Workforce

Job Workspace is Sri Lanka's premier multilingual platform bridging the gap between skilled daily-wage workers and households or businesses needing their services.

We believe that everyone deserves fair access to work opportunities. By leveraging technology, we are formalizing the informal sector, providing dignity, security, and consistent income for workers.

Our Mission

To create a seamless, transparent, and secure ecosystem where skilled workers can easily find employment and job providers can hire with confidence.

Our Vision

To be the leading digital platform for informal employment in South Asia, fostering economic growth and improving the livelihoods of millions.

What Sets Us Apart

Trilingual Support

Accessible in Sinhala, Tamil, and English to ensure no one is left behind.

AI Matching

Our smart algorithms consider location, skills, and ratings to find the perfect fit.

Direct Communication

Built-in chat and notification systems keep everyone in the loop.
