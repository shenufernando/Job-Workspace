Job Workspace User Manual

Everything you need to know to hire skilled professionals or find your next job opportunity.

Guide for Job Providers

  • 1

    Sign up as a Provider

    Create your account by selecting the "Provider" role. This gives you access to the dashboard where you can manage your hirings.

  • 2

    Post a New Job

    Provide clear details about the work required, the salary, and explicitly list the Required Skills so our AI can find the perfect match.

  • 3

    View AI-powered Best Matches

    Once the job is published, click "Matches" to see the workers our BERT AI algorithm has mathematically ranked based on skills, location, and experience.

  • 4

    Accept Applicants & Collaborate

    Review the applicants, accept the best candidate, and utilize the real-time Multimodal Chat (which supports Images and Voice) or initiate a seamless Video/Audio Call.

  • 5

    Complete the Job & Pay

    Once the work is done, click the "Complete" button to open the Payment Modal. Securely select "Cash" or "Card" to pay the worker's salary and finalize the project.

Guide for Workers

  • 1

    Sign up as a Worker

    Register as a skilled worker to explore hundreds of active daily-wage and remote job opportunities across the platform.

  • 2

    Complete Your Profile

    Update your personal details, upload a profile picture, and critically, add your Specific Skills. The AI uses this to rank you for jobs!

  • 3

    Browse Recommended Jobs

    Check the "Recommended" section on your dashboard. These jobs are mathematically proven to be a high match for your specific skillset.

  • 4

    Apply & Communicate

    Apply for the jobs you want. Once accepted, you can securely communicate with the provider via Chat or Video call directly through the workspace.

  • 5

    Get Hired & Paid

    Finish the job successfully. The provider will trigger the Secure Completion process and route your salary directly to you!
